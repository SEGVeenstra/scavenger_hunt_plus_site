Privacy Policy for Scavenger Hunt Plus

Last Updated: Feb 9 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Scavenger Hunt Plus, developed by SEGIT ("we," "us," or "our"). This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Device Information: We automatically collect certain device information for analytics purposes, such as device name, operating system name, and version. This information is collected by Firebase, our third-party analytics provider.

3. Purpose of Data Collection

We collect device information to improve the performance and user experience of Scavenger Hunt Plus. This data is used solely for analytics and is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

4. User Consent

By using the Scavenger Hunt Plus app, you consent to the collection and use of your device information for analytics purposes.

5. Third-Party Services

Scavenger Hunt Plus utilizes Firebase for analytics. Please review Firebase's privacy policy for information on how they handle data: Firebase Privacy Policy.

6. User-Generated Content

Scavenger Hunts are created by users and function offline. No personal information is required or collected during the creation or participation in Scavenger Hunts.

7. Age Restrictions

Scavenger Hunt Plus is intended for users of all ages, including kids. Parents or legal guardians are encouraged to supervise and guide their children while using the app.

8. Security Measures

We take appropriate measures to secure the collected device information. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app, and users are encouraged to review the policy periodically.

10. Contact Information

For privacy concerns or inquiries, please contact us at contact@segit.nl.
